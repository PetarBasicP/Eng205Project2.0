Themes

There are plenty of underlying themes in the movie Parasite, but I chose the four I thought were the most relevant and the ones that the director, Bong Joon-ho wanted to point out.

Class

One of the most evident and most consistent themes in this movie is class. We can clearly depict the differences between people based on their class and analyze them based on the class they are in.

  • Learn more

Parasitism

Without even seeing the movie, viewer's can guess what to expect based on the title of the movie.

  • Learn more

Violence

Violence in the movie is closely connected with class and desperation, since most of the violent attacks in the movie are ignited by some sort of inequality or jealousy of the poor.
